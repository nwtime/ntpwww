4.2.8p14 Release Announcement

Last update: February 11, 2022 20:40 UTC (b87954544)

The NTP Project at Network Time Foundation publicly released ntp-4.2.8p14 on Tuesday, 03 March 2020.

This release fixes three security issues in ntpd:

  • NONE: 3610: process_control() should bail earlier on short packets.
    • Reported by Philippe Antoine (Catena cyber with oss-fuzz).
  • MEDIUM: 3596: Unauthenticated ntpd may be susceptible to IPv4 spoof attack from highly predictable transmit timestamps.
    • Reported by Miroslav Lichvar.
  • MEDIUM: 3592: DoS Attack on unauthenticated client.
    • The fix for 3445 introduced a bug whereby a system that is running ntp-4.2.8p12 (possibly earlier) or p13 that only has one unauthenticated time source can be attacked in a way that causes the victim’s next poll to its source to be delayed, for as long as the attack is maintained.
    • Reported by Miroslav Lichvar.

and provides 46 bugfixes and addresses 4 other issues.

ENotification of these issues were delivered to our Institutional members on a rolling basis as they were reported and as progress was made.
